About Me

Where business thinking
meets human-centred design.

My journey started in Business Administration and Marketing — giving me an analytical lens that most designers don't carry. At PepsiCo I analysed P&L and ROI data; at Alles Brindes I turned that insight into visual strategy that drove 25% B2B sales growth.

I made the full pivot to UX/UI design because I realised the most impactful work lives at the intersection of data and empathy. Today I specialise in Design Systems, Prototyping, and Research — delivering both B2B and B2C solutions that go beyond aesthetics to solve real business problems.

In my most recent project (consulting for Banco BV), I led a 3-month redesign of an internal Customer Success platform used by 100+ operators, achieving measurable improvements in AHT and CSAT.
